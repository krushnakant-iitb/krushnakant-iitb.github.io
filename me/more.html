About Me

I joined IIT Bombay in 2019, in the Mechanical Engineering branch. Later, I changed my major to Computer Science and Engineering.

I did my JEE prep at IIT Home, a coaching Institute in Nagpur, Maharashtra while pursuing +2 from Dr. Ambedkar College Nagpur. (Fun?) Fact: This college is located exactly at DeekshaBhoomi's site, which is an important place for Buddhism in India in general, and Dr. Ambedkar's followers in particular.

I was born and raised at Khamgaon, where I also did my education till matriculation.

Outside of STEM, I am particularly interested in the social sciences, especially geopolitics and international affairs; alongwith Politics and Mythology. And ofcourse, I am a Potterhead! And yes, I also happen to particularly like the American TV Sitcoms Friends and HIMYM.
